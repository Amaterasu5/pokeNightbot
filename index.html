How to set up pokeNightbot


Just add all these commands to your nightbot

!data

$(eval $(urlfetch https://pokenightbot.herokuapp.com/?data=$(1)))

!learn

$(urlfetch https://pokenightbot.herokuapp.com/learn/?pokemon=$(1)&move=$(2))

!calc

attacker=$(querystring $(1))&defender=$(querystring $(2))&move=$(3)&field=$(querystring $(4))

!_calc (helper method for !calc)

$(eval $(urlfetch https://pokenightbot.herokuapp.com/calc/?$(query)))
